Our Story

We Believe Food is India's Greatest Story

Zaayaka was born in a Mumbai kitchen in 2022, from a simple argument: which city has the best street food? Three friends, zero agreement, and thousands of kilometres of eating later — this guide exists.

What we do

Your Honest, Independent Food Guide

We don't accept advertising. We don't take commissions from restaurants. Every dish, every spot, every city guide is researched in person by our team of food journalists and regional correspondents who actually live in these cities.

🔍
Verified Picks
Every restaurant and street food spot is visited, eaten at, and fact-checked by a local correspondent before it appears on Zaayaka.
🚫
Zero Paid Content
We accept no sponsorships, advertorial content, or placement fees. If it's on Zaayaka, it earned its place on taste alone.
🗺
Deep Regional Focus
We go beyond butter chicken. Our guides cover 34 states, 180+ regional cuisines, and sub-regional variations most guides miss entirely.
